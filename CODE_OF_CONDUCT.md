# Code of Conduct

This project is a respectful space for everyone. Here's what's expected:

## Do

- Be kind and constructive in discussions
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences
- Give credit where it's due
- Keep feedback focused on the code, not the person

## Don't

- Harass, insult, or demean other contributors
- Share anyone's private information with or without permission
- Spam or post promotional content
- Impersonate others

If you can't follow these basics, your contributions or access may be removed, or you may be banned from the project. Better yet, don't look at the repo, there are plenty of other projects to contribute to if you don't like it.
